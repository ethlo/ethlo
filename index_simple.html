Zally OpenAPI Maven plugin released - 27th of August, 2021

Working more and more with document-first APIs, and then naturally OpenAPI 3.x, the need for validating or linting the specification becomes important. Personally I agree with most of the rules that Zalando have decided, and thus really like their Zally offering.

However, it requires the setup of a service (web-api) and using either their CLI or web UI. This together with the fact that I often split my specification into multiple smaller fragments, it becomes a bid tedious to use, thus I developed a plugin for Maven that allows super simple validation and configuration of rules to run and what rules will fail the build if broken.

Check it out here: https://github.com/ethlo/zally-maven-plugin
